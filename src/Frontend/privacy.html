Evolve-X Privacy Policy

What we collect:

  • Your IP on certain requests. These requests include but are not limited to: Unauthorized admin requests, and requests to unknown endpoints.

  • Your username and user ID on certain requests. These include but are not limited to: Uploading/deleting files, verifying/denying accounts (admin), deleting your account or other accounts (admin), and shortening images

Why we collect this information

  • We collect this information to make Evolve-X safe for users and the host, and to hold you responsible for any action that can possibly deface the service or its hosts.

Who is this information shared to

  • Your user and user ID may be sent via a webhook to a discord channel.
    • Cont: This includes when you upload an image or shorten or take those things down, the url may be shared.
    - Your IP is never shared by Evolve-X.
Your IP may be kept within log files but it is never shared outside of Evolve-X by the service, your account information along with your shortened links and images is stored in a database that is accessible by service administrators.
